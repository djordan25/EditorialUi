Behavior

  • RequiredBuild on Radix DropdownMenu. Auto-flip placement; outside click and Esc dismiss.
  • RequiredArrow keys navigate. Enter or Space invokes. Type-ahead matches first letter.
  • RequiredSubmenus open on hover (200ms) or right-arrow / Enter.
  • RequiredMenu closes on item activation. Toggle items (checkable) keep menu open.
  • AvoidMenus with > 12 items. Group into submenus or restructure as a popover with sections.

Decision rules

  • UseOverflow / contextual actions on a row. Page-level action menus. Split button menus.
  • PreferEdContextMenu when triggered by right-click on an entity.
  • PreferEdSelect for picking a value (not invoking an action).
  • PreferEdPopover when the content isn't a list of commands.

A11y

  • RequiredTrigger: aria-haspopup="menu" + aria-expanded. Menu has role="menu"; items role="menuitem".
  • RequiredCheckable items use role="menuitemcheckbox" + aria-checked. Group labels use role="group".
  • RequiredDestructive items get an explicit aria-label that includes the entity ("Delete finding F-2438").
