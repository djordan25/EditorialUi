Decision rules

  • UseMulti-select inside a group ("any of these"). Table-row selection. Consent / policy acknowledgement.
  • UseIndeterminate for "select all" header when subset is selected. Click toggles all-on; click again toggles all-off.
  • AvoidFor binary on/off settings — use EdSwitch. Checkbox implies "selected for an action"; switch implies "this is now on".
  • AvoidFor mutually-exclusive choices — use EdRadioGroup.

A11y

  • RequiredWrap real <input type="checkbox"> visually-hidden under the label. Click target is the entire label.
  • RequiredIndeterminate set via ref.indeterminate = true in JS, plus aria-checked="mixed".
  • RequiredGroup with shared label uses <fieldset> + <legend>; legend may be visually hidden.
