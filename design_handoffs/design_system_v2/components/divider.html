Decision rules

  • UseSubtle separation where space and headings can't do the job (menus, dense metadata strips, timelines).
  • UseLabeled divider for time-based grouping in feeds and logs.
  • PreferWhitespace over a divider when content is already clearly structured.
  • PreferA heading (H2/H3) when the next region is a real section.
  • AvoidStacking dividers. Two adjacent dividers means you have spacing wrong.

A11y

  • RequiredUse <hr> for horizontal dividers between text blocks. Decorative dividers (menus, inline strips) use <div role="separator">.
  • RequiredVertical separator inside a metadata strip: aria-hidden="true" — screen readers read content sequentially anyway.
