Approval mode

Decision rules

  • Use2–5 mutually-exclusive choices, all visible at once.
  • UseVertical when descriptions are present or labels are long. Horizontal for 2–4 short labels.
  • PreferEdSelect for 6+ options or when space is tight.
  • AvoidBoolean (yes/no) — use EdCheckbox or EdSwitch. Two-radio yes/no is wasted space.
  • AvoidPre-selected destructive option. Default should be the safest, most-common choice.

A11y

  • RequiredUse Radix RadioGroup — handles arrow-key navigation and roving tabindex.
  • RequiredGroup label via <legend> or aria-labelledby.
  • RequiredTab into the group → first option focused; arrows move within; Tab leaves.
