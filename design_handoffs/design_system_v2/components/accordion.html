Decision rules

  • UseOptional / secondary content on a detail page. FAQ patterns. Long-form configuration grouped by category.
  • UseSingle-open by default. Multi-open when sections are unrelated and users may want to compare.
  • PreferEdTabs when sections are siblings (same level, mutually exclusive views).
  • AvoidAccordions inside dialogs. Modals should show full content.
  • AvoidHiding critical information. If a user needs it to do their job, don't make them click.

A11y

  • RequiredBuild on Radix Accordion. Trigger has aria-expanded + aria-controls. Panel has matching id.
  • RequiredTitle is the focusable, single-action element. Don't put nested buttons in the trigger row.
  • RequiredUp/Down arrows move between triggers; Home/End jump to first/last; Enter/Space toggles.
