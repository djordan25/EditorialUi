Decision rules

  • UseInline filter rows, segmented selectors with 2–4 options, removable input tokens in tag selects.
  • PreferEdFilterChipRow when you need ≥3 chips with counts, overflow, and standard wiring.
  • PreferEdTag for non-interactive metadata. Chips imply you can click them.
  • PreferEdButton for action verbs. Chips are for selection.

A11y

  • RequiredSelection chip: <button role="checkbox"> with aria-checked, or role="radio" for single-select group.
  • RequiredInput chip: focusable; Backspace / Delete removes it.
  • RequiredCount badge included in accessible name: aria-label="Open, 87 items".
