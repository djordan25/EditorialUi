Decision rules

  • UseMulti-tag forms — regulations, business units, owners, watchers, categories.
  • PreferEdComboBox (multi) when you don't want the inline tag display (e.g. compact dropdown trigger).
  • PreferEdCheckbox group when ≤6 fixed options.
  • AvoidFree-text creation on production-critical lists. Lock to known values.

A11y

  • RequiredBuild on Radix Combobox with multiple. Each selected tag is keyboard-focusable; Backspace from empty input removes last.
  • RequiredEach tag's close button has aria-label="Remove {tag}".
  • RequiredLive region announces additions/removals: "{tag} added. 3 of 12 selected."
